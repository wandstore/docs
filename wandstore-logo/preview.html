✦ Recommendations

  • Primary Logo: Concept 1 (Wand Stroke) — Most versatile, distinctive, and scalable. Captures the magic metaphor elegantly.
  • Secondary/Icon: Concept 3 (Generative Spark) — Excellent for favicon, app icon, and small applications. Recognizable at 16px.
  • Wordmark: Concept 4 — For formal contexts where full brand name is needed. Professional with subtle brand personality.
  • Special Applications: Concept 2 for hero sections; Concept 5 for developer docs and technical contexts.
